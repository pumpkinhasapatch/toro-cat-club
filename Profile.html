- Toro cats originated from a handheld video game system called "Pocket Station".

-This is Sony Computer Entertainment's latest concept game, released on July 22, 1999.

-This game is named "Doko Demo Issyo" ----- Together wherever we go!

-The initial idea was to create a game that girls would want to play, so they came up with the idea of a fun way to "make it talk"!

-You can teach it to talk, and slowly it will start talking to you, like making a good friend.

      It often makes you feel warm inside, and sometimes it will even say things that make you blush and your heart race!

-Whether you're a child, an adult, a boy, a girl, or someone who doesn't like video games, you'll want to try it. This is software that makes everyone happy.

-"Pocket Station" also has a communication function, allowing you to communicate with your friends.
